Can your potential customers find you on the Internet?
Search is the answer! With 83% of all users using the Internet to search for products and services and 35% of those searches having local intent, it’s easy to understand the importance of having a search strategy, both long and short term. There are 3 common components to search marketing to maximize 100% of the SERP. (search engine results page)

SEO: SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. This is the act of enhancing a website so it ranks higher on major search engines like Google, Yahoo, and Bing. Google uses over 200 grading factors when determining what websites to rank. The wide majority of the factors deal with how relevant and popular the website is in relationship to the keywords they are targeting. Improving a website’s relevance and popularity will help it rank higher. Create multiple, highly targeted landing pages based on the most common keyword searches. Each page is SEO optimized on the backend to increase traffic and drive more leads to your business.

Maps Optimization: Maps Optimization is the act of enhancing a local Google Map Listing so it receives more exposure when someone conducts a local search. It’s considered to be the most important method involved within a local online marketing campaign. Google grades local businesses according to three major factors: location, relevance, and popularity. Having a precise location and optimizing a businesses relevance and popularity will increase their exposure in the maps section.

PPC: PPC stands for pay per click. This is the act of renting search engine ad space on a per click basis. It is the quickest way to drive traffic to a website. Naturally obtaining front page rankings is cumbersome. This is why major search engines allow businesses to rent ad space on their front page. This allows businesses to bid for placement and compete for their targeted keywords. Having a structured PPC campaign allows you to maximize your exposure and ROI. At DLC Marketing Group, our fully automated engine allows you to create and execute geo-targeted paid search simply by entering in the business name and URL. Our system automatically creates a high quality ad based on similar, high-performing ads and then traffics and dynamically adjusts bidding to optimize ROI.
