Text Messaging For Success

In today’s fast-paced modern society, businesses need to keep up with upcoming trends, technologies, and (most importantly) customers. Many businesses spend so much time, money and effort to get customers to walk in but how do you communicate with them once they leave your establishment?

Communicating to your customers in this preferred manner is pivotal for any good business to thrive. With the rise of social media, instant messaging, chatbots, and email marketing, it’s easy to overlook text messaging as a powerful form of communication for businesses in 2020.

Text Messaging Statistics 2020 In USA

Statistics have shown that 98% of all text messages are opened, and 95% of text messages are opened and responded to within 3 minutes of being delivered.

Additionally, 9 out of 10 consumers would like to communicate with businesses through text message, whether this be through sending alerts, reminders and 2-way communication. Moreover, 75% of customers would like special offers sent to them via text message. The conversion rate for these sorts of text messages are much higher (9.18%), compared to other marketing channels such as Facebook (0.90%) and Google Adwords (1.91%).
